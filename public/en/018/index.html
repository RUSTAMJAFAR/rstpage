ssh: permissions

here’s a small list of permissions that ssh wants you to follow

  • .ssh directory: 700 (drwx------)

  • public key: 644 (rw-r—​r--)

  • private key: 600 (rw-------)

  • home directory: 755 (drwxr-xr-x)
