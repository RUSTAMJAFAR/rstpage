IN THE NAME OF GOD, THE MOST BENEFICENT, THE MOST MERCIFUL

prophet (pbuh): deeds are only by intentions

you’re not w/out talent, you’re w/out direction

i think, that’s the first thing you have to consider when starting something new. this doesn’t matter your color, your ethnic background, your social status or your education. this will definitely help you to set the direction of your journey through life.

the following are a few questions that i think, everyone should ask themselves:

  • why do i start doing what i start to do?

  • who will benefit from what i start to do?

  • what will the future look like?

sure, each of us will have different answers to these questions, based on our values. but, here is the thing. each of us will have noticed that our answers change as we grow as a person. so ask yourself these questions frequently, for it will help you to adjust your direction.
